## 1. DESCRIPTION
Subject-004-USA refers to the "Redwood Corporate Plaza," an 8-story brutalist office complex that served as the secret headquarters for the *Aetheric Research Group (ARG)* until their sudden dissolution in 1989. 

The building is physically decaying, but its internal systems (lights, elevators, computers) continue to operate sporadically without a power source. The site is haunted by "The Staff"â€”a collective of spectral entities dressed in scorched 1980s business attire.

## 2. ANOMALOUS PROPERTIES
* **The Overtime Protocol:** Any human entering the building after 6:00 PM local time becomes subject to "The Shift." All exits vanish and are replaced by infinite cubicle rows. The subject feels an overwhelming psychological urge to sit at a desk and begin typing on non-functional keyboards.
* **The CEO (Subject-004-A):** A massive, shadowy entity residing in the penthouse office. It is composed of magnetic tape from thousands of destroyed VHS cassettes. It does not speak but emits a high-pitched mechanical screech that causes permanent hearing loss.
* **Temporal Leakage:** Documents found within the building often describe events that have not yet occurred, or "alternative" history where the Bureau was never formed.

## 3. INCIDENT LOG
- **1989-10-14:** The ARG headquarters goes dark. Local police find the building empty, but all coffee mugs were still steaming hot. No bodies were ever recovered.
- **2002-05-20:** Urban explorers entered the 3rd floor. One survivor was found three days later inside a sealed ventilation duct, claiming the "Manager" wanted to review his performance.
- **2013-11-11:** Bureau cleanup crews attempted to recover the ARG Mainframe. They were forced to retreat when the elevators began opening into a void filled with human eyes.

## 4. CONTAINMENT PROTOCOL
The site is designated as a **Permanent Exclusion Zone**.
* **Physical Barrier:** A 4-meter high concrete wall has been erected around the plaza. 
* **Acoustic Dampening:** High-powered speakers play white noise 24/7 to drown out the "screech" from the penthouse.
* **Counter-Intrusion:** Any individual seen attempting to enter the building is to be detained and administered Class-B Amnestics. Under no circumstances should any personnel answer the ringing payphones near the entrance.
